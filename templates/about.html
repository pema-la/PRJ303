About Us

Who We Are?

A Drung-Tshab system for medical institutions and patients to predict Disease and through chatting with our system can also general minor Disease for their use. Drung-Tshab started off as a PRJ303 that formed strong relationships with medical institutions and patients to help them generate their Diseases with ease and maximum efficiency thus saving the organization's valuable time.



Our Heart

Vision:

To help medical institutions and patients to detect diseases early and recommend treatment according to the prediction result.

Mission:

  • To help patients in early disease detection and reduce checkup process
  • To help medical institutions
  • To save time
  • To save human resources and healthcost
