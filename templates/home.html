Disease Prediction

Disease prediction applications can play an important role in promoting early detection, prevention, and management of diseases. Implementing machine learning in healthcare can lead to a significant increase in patient satisfaction. Instead of directly treating the patient, predicting the disease beforehand with machine learning algorithms can make the entire treatment process much more efficient.

A Drung-Tshab system for medical institutions and patients to predict Disease and through chatting with our system can also general minor Disease for their use. Drung-Tshab started off as a PRJ303 that formed strong relationships with medical institutions and patients to help them generate their Diseases with ease and maximum efficiency thus saving the organization's valuable time.
